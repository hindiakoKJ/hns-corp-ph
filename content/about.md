## The Company

HNScorpPH is a solo-founded Philippine company. We build software for two audiences that foreign tech companies systematically underserve: small businesses trying to stay BIR-compliant, and families navigating health conditions without a safety net.

The company is currently in the process of SEC OPC (One Person Corporation) registration.

---

## Why We Build This

Most business software sold in the Philippines is either too expensive, too generic, or too foreign. It doesn't know what a 2550Q is. It doesn't have a field for GCash settlement dates. It assumes your internet is always on and your accountant is down the hall.

Filipino MSMEs run on receipt pads, spreadsheets, and one accountant who visits once a quarter. That works — until there's an audit, a cash shortfall, or a staff member who quits and takes the spreadsheet with them.

On the health side, epilepsy management tools are either clinical (expensive, English-only, not built for low-resource settings) or non-existent. A family in Davao managing epilepsy shouldn't have to pay for a subscription app to log their seizures.

We build the tools that should exist but don't. Starting with the ones where we know the problem firsthand.

---

## The Products

**Clerque** — A business suite for Filipino MSMEs: point of sale, BIR-compliant accounting, OR sequential numbering, and AP/AR tracking. Built for the compliance requirements of the Philippine market, with an offline-capable POS for real-world connectivity.

**Steady** — A free mobile companion for people living with epilepsy. Seizure logging, BEACON emergency alerts, first-aid bystander guide, and neurologist PDF reports. Free, private, built with care.
