## Company (BrandSheet section)

**Section label:** HNScorpPH · Brand System v1

**Heading:**  
One parent. A growing family of products.  
HNScorpPH is the platform — Clerque and Steady are the first to ship.

**Footer columns:**

**Column 1 — Founded in the Philippines**  
HNScorpPH OPC is a solo-founded Philippine company building digital infrastructure for local businesses and families. Every product is designed around Filipino workflows, BIR compliance requirements, and real-world connectivity constraints — not adapted from foreign software.

**Column 2 — Two products live. More coming.**  
Clerque is the flagship business suite — point of sale, BIR-compliant accounting, and AP/AR tracking built for MSMEs. Steady is a free health companion for people living with epilepsy and their caregivers, offering seizure logging, BEACON emergency alerts, and neurologist PDF reports.

**Column 3 — One mark. One promise.**  
Every HNScorpPH product carries the same commitment: built local, built right, built to last. The accent color changes with each product. The promise to Filipino businesses and families doesn't.
